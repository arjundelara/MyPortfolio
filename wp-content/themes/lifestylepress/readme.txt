## Description

LifeStylePress – is pure and simple wordpress blog theme for all fans of beautiful design and flexible functionality. LifeStylePress is created to emphasize individuality and to focus on creativity and independence of bloggers. LifeStylePress is a powerful tool with truly elegant design.
